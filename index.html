Z Text

A text-based zombie survival game

Inventory

    Current Location:

    Grab: Places an item in inventory.

    Move: Moves you to an adjoining room.

    Set: Sets an item in a defensive position.

    Use: Wields an item.

    Combine: Combines two items to create a new item.

    Look: Looks around the room and gives a description.

    Examine: Examines an item and gives a detailed description.
